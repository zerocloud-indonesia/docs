Want to create your dream server at ZeroCloud? Follow these steps to buy balance and order hosting.

## How to Buy Balance at ZeroCloud Indonesia

<Steps>
   <Step title="Register or Login to ZeroCloud Billing">
      Register or login to your account at [billing.zerocloud.id](https://billing.zerocloud.id).
      ![Billing Login Screen](/images/login.gif)
   </Step>
   <Step title="Open Store Menu">
      Open the **Store** menu, select the amount of balance you want to top up, then press the **Purchase** button.
      ![Store Menu](/images/store.png)
   </Step>
   <Step title="Choose Payment Method">
      Choose your favorite payment method (Midtrans/QRIS) and click **Submit Payment**.
      ![Payment Method](/images/payment.png)
   </Step>
   <Step title="Make Payment">
      A QRIS will appear that you can scan directly. Make the payment according to the instructions.
      ![QRIS Payment](/images/qris.gif)
      If the payment is successful, your balance will be automatically updated.
   </Step>
</Steps>

## How to Order a Server at ZeroCloud

<Steps>
   <Step title="Open Create Server Menu">
      Go to the **Create Server** menu in the ZeroCloud panel.
      ![Create Server Menu](/images/create-server.png)
   </Step>
   <Step title="Fill in Server Details">
      Fill in the server name and other parameters according to your needs.
      ![Create Server Form](/images/server-form.png)
   </Step>
   <Step title="Choose Software/Games">
      Choose **Software/Games** and set the default to **Onboarding** (can be changed later in the panel).
      ![Choose Software/Games](/images/software-selection.png)
   </Step>
   <Step title="Choose Node">
      Choose an available Node. Make sure the stock is sufficient before proceeding.
      ![Choose Node](/images/node-selection.png)
   </Step>
   <Step title="Confirm for Private Node">
      For Private Node, please confirm through a ticket.
   </Step>
   <Step title="Complete the Order">
      Your server will be ready to use. Once completed, press the **Manage** button or login to the Control Panel to start exploring your server.
   </Step>
</Steps>
