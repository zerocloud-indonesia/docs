# What is ZeroCloud?

ZeroCloud Indonesia is a hosting service provider focused on **high performance**, **best security**, and **affordable prices**. We provide reliable hosting solutions for various needs, including **Minecraft**, **Discord servers**, and other **cloud applications**. Our services are supported by the latest technology and customer support ready to assist you anytime.

ZeroCloud is designed to **simplify your server management**, provide **automatic security**, and offer **resources** that can be customized according to the specific needs of users. Whether it's for **game hosting**, **cloud services**, or **application development**, ZeroCloud is here to provide a better experience in the digital world.

ZeroCloud also prioritizes **ease of access**, with a **user-friendly control panel** equipped with various advanced features such as **auto-backup**, **AI integration**, and various service options that can be tailored to your budget and needs.

This page is a **knowledge base** for ZeroCloud and related services. This page provides general guides on how to use our services, basic troubleshooting, and various other guides! If you want to contribute, you can submit changes to a branch here and request any documents to be merged on [Github](https://github.com/ZeroCloudIndonesia).

<Info>ZeroCloud Indonesia always strives to provide the **best hosting experience** with **high performance** and **affordable prices**. Whether it's hosting for Minecraft, Discord servers, or custom applications, we always provide **full support** to ensure your server runs smoothly without interruption.</Info>
